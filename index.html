    process 1

    Material Selection

    We carefully choose every metal, stone, and component before crafting begins. Only quality, skin-safe, and durable materials are used to ensure comfort and longevity.

    Each piece is shaped, cut, and assembled by hand using traditional tools and techniques. No mass production—every item receives individual attention.

    After shaping, every piece is carefully polished and finished to achieve a smooth, refined look. This step enhances shine, comfort, and overall quality.

    Each piece is inspected to ensure it meets our quality standards. It is then gently packed in protective, elegant packaging—ready to reach you safely.
